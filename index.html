How It Works

Weekly Competition Rules

  • Watch a minimum of 5 ads to qualify for the leaderboard
  • Each ad view is counted and recorded in our secure database
  • The competition resets every Sunday at midnight
  • The user who watched the most ads during the week wins
  • Winners will be contacted via email for prize distribution
  • Multiple accounts per person are not allowed

Getting Started

  1. Create an account with a valid email address
  2. Log in to your account
  3. Navigate to your profile page
  4. Click the "Watch Ads" button
  5. Complete watching the ad (30 seconds)
  6. Your progress will automatically be tracked

Prize Information

Every week, we announce winners based on their position on the leaderboard:

  • 1st Place: Main prize of the week
  • 2nd Place: Runner-up prize
  • 3rd Place: Smaller prize
  • All participants who watched at least 5 ads are eligible for random bonus prizes

Prizes vary each week and will be announced on the homepage.
